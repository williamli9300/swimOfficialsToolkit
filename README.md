<blockquote>
  <b>Instructions for Installing HyTek Custom DQ Codes:</b><br>
    • Copy <code>dqcodesCustom.txt</code> to your <code>C:\Hy-Sport\SwMM7</code> or <code>C:\Hy-Sport\SwMM8</code> folder.<br>
    • Open Hy-Tek Swim Meet Manager. In the home screen, navigate to <code>Set-up</code> > <code>Meet Set-up</code>. <br>
    • In the bottom right of the Meet Set-Up screen, select <b>Custom DQ Codes</b> from the <b>DQ Codes</b> dropdown.
</blockquote>
